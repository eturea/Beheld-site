Device requirements

Base app
iPhone with iOS 26 or later.
Personal AI writing
An Apple Intelligence-capable iPhone.
Postcard images
An Apple Intelligence-capable iPhone with Image Playground available. Beheld falls back to bundled illustrations elsewhere.

Permissions

Beheld asks for permission to read movement data from Apple Health. The data is read on your iPhone and never uploaded.

Purchases

The one-time $4.99 Personalization unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. To restore the purchase on a new iPhone signed in to the same Apple ID, open Beheld, go to Settings → Restore Purchase.

Frequently asked

Why doesn't Beheld say "walked" or "ran"?
Because some movement isn't walking or running. The companion uses mode-neutral verbs — covered, went, gained, returned — so the journal works for any way you move through the world.

What if I don't move very much one day?
That's fine. Rest is part of the record, not its absence. The companion writes about quiet days too.

Can I export my journal?
Journal entries are stored on your iPhone alongside the rest of your data. They are included in your iPhone's standard iCloud backup if enabled.
