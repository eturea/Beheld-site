Privacy

Effective May 2026

Beheld is designed to work on your iPhone. Your movement history is used to write your journal and is not uploaded to Beheld servers. On supported devices, Apple Intelligence features run on device. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

Health data

Beheld reads HealthKit movement data with your permission. That data stays on your iPhone. Beheld does not upload your movement history to Beheld, to an ad network, or to any third-party server.

Writing

Where Apple Intelligence is available, your journal text is generated on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. When Personalization is enabled, your movement data is included in the prompt that runs locally — the prompt is not sent to Beheld.

Postcard images

Where Image Playground is available, milestone postcard images are generated on your device. Generated images are stored locally on your iPhone in your Beheld journal. When on-device generation is not available, Beheld uses bundled illustrations.

Purchases

The one-time Personalization purchase is processed by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. Beheld receives a receipt from Apple confirming the purchase; Beheld does not receive payment card details, billing address, or other payment information.

No account

Beheld does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no Beheld profile, no email collection, and no sync server.

No analytics

Beheld does not use any external analytics SDK, attribution SDK, or tracking pixel. Beheld does not measure feature usage on remote servers.

Contact

For questions about this policy, email support@beheld.day.
